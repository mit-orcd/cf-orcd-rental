About Custom SKUs

Custom SKUs are typically used for:

  • QoS Tiers - Custom Quality of Service configurations
  • Special Subscriptions - Custom maintenance packages

Node SKUs are automatically created from NodeType records and cannot be manually created.

Important
  • SKU codes are permanent and cannot be changed
  • All custom SKUs use monthly billing
  • Once created, SKUs can be deactivated but not deleted
